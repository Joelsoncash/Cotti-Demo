Today's Math Lesson: Addition and Subtraction

We will learn about basic addition and subtraction with numbers 1-20.

Key Concepts:
1. Adding numbers together
2. Subtracting numbers
3. Understanding number relationships
4. Counting forward and backward
5. Using number lines for calculations

Practice Problems:
- What is 5 + 3?
- If you have 10 apples and eat 4, how many are left?
- Count from 1 to 20
- Add 7 + 8

Remember to use your fingers or draw pictures if it helps!
